Job Title: Direct Sales Representative

Job Summary

We are seeking a motivated and results-driven Direct Sales Representative to join our team. The role focuses on agent recruitment, lines distribution, and fleet servicing to drive sales growth and ensure excellent customer service. Candidates from high school, college, or university backgrounds are welcome, with qualifications and expectations tailored to their educational level.

Job Qualifications

1. High School Graduate

  • Education: High School Diploma or equivalent.
  • Experience: No prior experience required, but experience in sales, customer service, or community engagement is a plus.
  • Skills:
    • Basic communication and interpersonal skills.
    • Ability to learn quickly and follow instructions.
    • Basic understanding of sales principles or willingness to be trained.
    • Familiarity with basic computer tools (e.g., email, spreadsheets) is an advantage.
  • Attributes:
    • Enthusiastic, self-motivated, and eager to meet new people.
    • Strong work ethic and ability to work under supervision.
    • Reliable and punctual with a positive attitude.

2. College Graduate (Diploma or Associate Degree)

  • Education: Diploma or Associate Degree in Sales, Marketing, Business, or a related field.
  • Experience: 1-2 years of experience in sales, customer service, or team coordination preferred.
  • Skills:
    • Strong verbal and written communication skills.
    • Ability to negotiate and persuade potential agents or clients.
    • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel) and CRM tools.
    • Basic knowledge of logistics or distribution processes is a plus.
  • Attributes:
    • Goal-oriented with a focus on achieving sales targets.
    • Ability to work independently and in a team.
    • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.

Key Roles and Responsibilities

  • Agent Recruitment:
    • Identify, recruit, and onboard new sales agents to expand the company's network.
    • Conduct interviews, assess candidates' suitability, and provide initial training.
    • Build and maintain relationships with agents to ensure long-term engagement and performance.
    • Monitor agent performance and provide ongoing support and motivation.
  • Lines Distribution:
    • Manage the distribution of product lines to agents, retailers, or clients.
    • Ensure timely and accurate delivery of products to meet demand.
    • Coordinate with logistics teams to optimize distribution processes.
    • Track inventory levels and report on distribution performance.
  • Fleet Servicing:
    • Oversee the maintenance and servicing of the company's fleet to ensure operational efficiency.
    • Coordinate with service providers to schedule regular vehicle maintenance and repairs.
    • Monitor fleet performance and ensure compliance with safety and regulatory standards.
    • Address any fleet-related issues promptly to minimize downtime.

Additional Requirements

  • Availability: Willingness to work flexible hours, including weekends or evenings, as needed.
  • Mobility: Ability to travel locally or regionally for recruitment, distribution, or fleet-related tasks.
  • Technology: Comfortable using mobile apps, GPS systems, or other tools for tracking and reporting.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary with performance-based.
  • Training and development opportunities.
  • Career growth potential within the organization.
  • Supportive team environment.
